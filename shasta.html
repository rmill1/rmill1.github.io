Shasta's Sleep Diary

A Compendium of Shasta's Favorite Sleeping Locations

Location 1: In my cat bed

Shasta's Rating: 10/10

The newest addition to the roster of sleeping spots, the cat bed was a Christmas present from my grandhumans. I did a good job of sleeping in it for a month, but now I basically ignore it unless nobody is watching.

Pros
  • Super fuzzy
  • If I forget where my fur ends and the bed begins, I can just lick the bed
  • The white fuzzy fabric is like built-in camouflage
Cons
  • Kind of cliche
  • Makes me seem like an average cat
    (I am not an average cat)

Location 2: Literally anywhere on the carpet

Shasta's Rating: 11/10

Arguably the most versatile of the sleeping locations. I can sleep anywhere and everywhere. On the carpet is also great for setting belly traps for my human.

Pros
  • Lots of space = lots of possibilities
  • I can make it seem like I want belly scratches
    (I don't want belly scratches)
  • Also great for practicing yoga
Cons
  • Not as ergonomic as other sleeping options

Location 3: On any blanket, but especially this fuzzy one

Shasta's Rating: 12/10

My human loves bonus blankets so there is nearly always a blanket around for me to claim as my own. One time, there was even a glorious blanket stack!

Pros
  • Super fuzzy
  • There's almost always a blanket around, so no pre-planning required
  • Extra fuzzy blankets also mean making biscuits!
Cons
  • ???

Location 4: On any blanket, especially ESPECIALLY when it's unfolded

Shasta's Rating: 13/10

Did I mention how much I love blankets? Well, unfolded is truly the best and I love all the extra unfolded cozy snuggliness. Plus the longer I lay here the more likely my human will never fold up the blanket.

Pros
  • STILL super fuzzy
  • No fold no problem
  • Extra super snuggly
  • Fits all of my curves
Cons
  • See above for blanket cons
